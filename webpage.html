Cats vs. Zombies:

Destroy the Infected!

Basic Description

A cute, yet dangerous adventure about saving all of cat-kind from an alien apocalypse!!!

DO NOT LET THEM INFECT YOU!

  • The enemy crew consists of a bunch of enemies with different abilities and attack strategies.
  • Magician casts spells from a medium distance
  • Killer deals medium up-close damage with bleeding
  • Thief can steal a random item from your inventory
  • Sharky can deal heavy up-close damage by amerging from underneath
  • Firebird deals medium damage with burning by striking from above
  • The Player on the other hand, comes eqquiped with a hazmat suit (so you don't get infected!), and a cool space-themed gun.
